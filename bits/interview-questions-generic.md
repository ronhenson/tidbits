# Interview Questions Generic
h2><strong>Basic interview questions:</strong></h2>

<ul>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/intreview-tell-me-about-yourself" target="_blank">Tell me about yourself</a>.</li>
	<li>What are your <a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/focus-on-your-strengths" target="_blank">strengths</a>?</li>
	<li>What are your <a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/recruiter-roundtable-the-weakness-question-hot-jobs" target="_blank">weaknesses</a>?</li>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/how-to-answer-question-why-do-I-want-this-job" target="_blank">Why do you want this job?</a></li>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/how-to-answer-job-interview-question-where-do-want-to-be-in-5-years" target="_blank">Where would you like to be in your career five years from now?</a></li>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/how-to-answer-job-interview-question-your-ideal-company" target="_blank">What&#39;s your ideal company?</a></li>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/how-to-answer-question-why-do-I-want-to-work-here" target="_blank">What attracted you to this company?</a></li>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/why-should-we-hire-you" target="_blank">Why should we hire you?</a></li>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/like-least-about-your-last-job" target="_blank">What did you like least about your last job?</a></li>
	<li>When were you most satisfied in your job?</li>
	<li>What can you do for us that other candidates can&#39;t?&nbsp;</li>
	<li>What were the responsibilities of your last position?&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/why-did-you-leave" target="_blank">Why are you leaving your present job?</a></li>
	<li>What do you know about this industry?&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
	<li>What do you know about our company?&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Are you willing to relocate?&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/interview-do-you-have-any-questions" target="_blank">Do you have any questions for me?</a></li>
</ul>

<h2><strong><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/you-can-survive-the-behavioral-interview" target="_blank">Behavioral interview questions</a>:</strong></h2>

<ul>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/how-to-answer-job-interview-question-what-was-last-project-led-outcome" target="_blank">What was the last project you led, and what was its outcome?</a>&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Give me an example of a time that you felt you went above and beyond the call of duty at work.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Can you describe a time when your work was criticized?&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Have you ever been on a team where someone was not pulling their own weight? How did you handle it?&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Tell me about a time when you had to give someone difficult feedback. How did you handle it?&nbsp;</li>
	<li>What is your greatest failure, and what did you learn from it?&nbsp;</li>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/how-to-answer-job-interview-question-working-with-annoying-people" target="_blank">How do you handle working with people who annoy you?</a>&nbsp;</li>
	<li>If I were your supervisor and asked you to do something that you disagreed with, what would you do?</li>
	<li>What was the most difficult period in your life, and how did you deal with it?&nbsp;</li>
	<li>Give me an example of a time you did something wrong. <a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/how-to-answer-job-interview-question-when-did-do-something-wrong-0922" target="_blank">How did you handle it?&nbsp;</a></li>
	<li>Tell me about a time where you had to deal with conflict on the job.&nbsp;</li>
	<li>If you were at a business lunch and you ordered a rare steak and they brought it to you well done, what would you do?&nbsp;</li>
	<li>If you found out your company was doing something against the law, like fraud, what would you do?&nbsp;</li>
	<li>What assignment was too difficult for you, and how did you resolve the issue?&nbsp;</li>
	<li>What&#39;s the most difficult decision you&#39;ve made in the last two years and how did you come to that decision?</li>
	<li>Describe how you would handle a situation if you were required to finish multiple tasks by the end of the day, and there was no conceivable way that you could finish them.&nbsp;</li>
</ul>

<h2><strong>Salary questions:</strong></h2>

<ul>
	<li>What salary are you seeking?</li>
	<li><a href="https://www.monster.com/career-advice/article/what-is-your-salary-history" target="_blank">What&#39;s your salary history?</a></li>
	<li>If I were to give you this salary you requested but let you write your job description for the next year, what would it say?</li>
</ul>

<h2><strong>Career development questions:</strong></h2>

<ul>
	<li>What are you looking for in terms of career development?&nbsp;</li>
	<li>How do you want to improve yourself in the next year?</li>
	<li>What kind of goals would you have in mind if you got this job?&nbsp;</li>
	<li>If I were to ask your last supervisor to provide you additional training or exposure, what would she suggest?</li>
</ul>

<h2><strong>Getting started questions:</strong></h2>

<ul>
	<li>How would you go about establishing your credibility quickly with the team?</li>
	<li>How long will it take for you to make a significant contribution?</li>
	<li>What do you see yourself doing within the first 30 days of this job?</li>
	<li>If selected for this position, can you describe your strategy for the first 90 days?</li>
</ul>
